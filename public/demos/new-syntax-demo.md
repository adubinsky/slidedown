## The Power of Symbols

- ^^^ `^^^` means "fade up" (arrows up)
- vvv `vvv` means "fade down" (arrows down)
- ---> `--->` means "slide right" (arrow right)
- <--- `<---` means "slide left" (arrow left)
- +++ `+++` means "grow" (getting bigger)
- ... `...` means "shrink" (fading away)

Intuitive and visual! +++
